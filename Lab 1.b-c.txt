Structural Tags:

<aside>: Used for the W3C Warning box
<article>: Wraps all the main content about page structuring
<nav>: Has site navigation menu with links to different pages
<section>: Divides the article into three topics

PART C
<header role="banner">: Contains the site title (h1), subtitle (h2), and navigation - appears at the top
<main role="main">: Wraps the primary content of the page (the article)
<footer role="contentinfo">: Contains site-wide footer information (CSUN address)

ARIA Roles Used:

role="banner" - identifies the header as the site banner
role="navigation" - identifies the nav as the navigation area
role="main" - identifies the main content area
role="complementary" - identifies the aside as supplementary content
role="contentinfo" - identifies the footer as site information
